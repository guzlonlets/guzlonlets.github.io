Vision 

Our vision is to be a world-class zero-to-landfill specialist eWaste Recycling Facility focusing on Africa.

Mission

Our mission is to be a center of excellence for eWaste recycling where:
eWaste is demanufactured in an environmentally friendly manner utilising state-of-the-art green recycling technologies and international best practices


We provide our customers with exceptional eWaste collection, recycling and disposal services 
We live the ideology of reduce, reuse, recycle
Our output is of the highest purity and quality
We strive to accomplish our mission statement through our core values.
